About Us

Malkeet Tubewell Works is a trusted name in tubewell drilling and water solution services. With years of hands-on experience, we specialize in providing reliable, durable, and efficient tubewell engineering solutions.

Our work is based on technical knowledge, practical experience, and a strong commitment to quality. We have successfully completed projects for agricultural lands, residential areas, industrial sites, and infrastructure foundations.

Our Experience

We have extensive experience in deep tubewell drilling, rotary ring systems, diamond and mineral drilling, dam foundation work, and pump installations. Every project is handled with care, precision, and responsibility.

Our commitment

We believe in honest work, long-term reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our focus is always on delivering strong and lasting water solutions using proper methods and quality equipment.
